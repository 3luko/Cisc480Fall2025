Non-Computer Science Project

Second Harvest Heartland Volunteering

Second Harvest Heartland is one of the largest food banks in the Midwest, dedicated to fighting hunger by distributing millions of meals each year to individuals and families in need. During my time volunteering there, I worked with a team to sort, pack, and prepare food items such as potatoes and onions for distribution. I also helped load pallets and organize goods for transport, ensuring efficiency and accuracy in the packing process. This experience allowed me to contribute directly to supporting my community while learning about the logistics and teamwork behind large-scale food distribution.

Relation to Capstone

Volunteering at Second Harvest Heartland connected to my capstone by reinforcing the importance of using technology and teamwork to address real-world challenges.
